About

A group of ten friends came together with one powerful idea—to host an event that celebrates startups of every kind. For its inaugural event, the Commit Festival launches in the city of Seoul, where innovators, entrepreneurs, artists, technology lovers, and others can come together to celebrate the transition of ideas into action and projects into progress. Come join us with your ideas, dreams, or products. Ready or not, come and commit!
